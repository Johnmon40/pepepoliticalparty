Ambassador of Russia

Alex’s appointment as Ambassador to Russia was more than political—it was poetic. A man forged in shadows and sharpened by scams, Alex earned his stripes decoding rug pulls and meme patterns long before his diplomatic handshake with President Pepe.

His love for the Russian mountains runs deep, where he often disappears for days with nothing but a backpack, a cigarette, and a case of local beer—returning with new wisdom, frost on his coat, and a viral meme idea in hand.

To the world, he’s a silent observer. But behind those eyes is a radar calibrated for deception, a sixth sense for grift, and a soul that reveres the raw, icy beauty of Russia like it’s a sacred altar.

From deciphering Ponzi schemes in the gulag of Telegram groups to sipping Baltika atop frozen cliffs while laughing at the absurdity of it all—Alex is the bridge between power and parody. A guardian of Pepe’s interests abroad, and the only man trusted to meme and mediate at a global scale.
