Before he was the President, he was just Doc—an unhinged meme degen riding leverage like a cowboy and sniffing out rugs like a bloodhound on bath salts. Legend says he once turned 12 cents into a Lambo and back into 7 cents on a single perp trade. The world ignored him. The charts didn’t. Doc Pepe rose from telegram admin to island lord, hosting summits with crypto elites, warlords, and influencers alike. Now, with the full weight of the $PPP behind him, Doc Pepe leads not with policy—but with memes, mischief, and unshakable meme-fueled destiny.
