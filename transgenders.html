Department of Transgenders - Ichi

Ichi earned his role as Head of the Department of Transgenders through a bold blend of activism, innovation, and unapologetic flair. Originally forged in the fires of fringe art and underground politics, Ichi rose to prominence by championing inclusivity with style—part visionary, part provocateur.

His unique ability to unify diverse voices under one gear-grinding vision caught President Pepe’s attention, especially after Ichi organized the now-iconic “Eyes Forward” campaign that turned national attention to trans rights using powerful symbolism and street art.

With a sharp mind, a sharper wit, and eyes that quite literally never blink, Ichi was the only choice. His appointment wasn’t just a promotion—it was a revolution.
