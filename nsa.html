NSA Director SR

SR’s rise to NSA Director was anything but conventional. Once a low-profile cryptography savant known only by his initials on dark web forums, SR gained underground fame for exposing backdoors in major global surveillance systems—all while sipping coffee in a bathrobe.

When President Pepe saw the viral thread titled “NSA Who?” authored by SR, he knew he’d found his guy. With unmatched digital espionage skills, a diamond-studded watch, and a smile that said “I read your texts before you did,” SR was fast-tracked past bureaucrats and sworn in with one firm handshake and a national firewall upgrade.

As the new Director, he promised one thing: "They’re watching. But now so are we."
